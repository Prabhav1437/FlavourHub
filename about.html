OUR STORY

What started as a small cloud kitchen in 2020 has blossomed into one of Mumbai's most beloved vegetarian restaurants. Our founder, inspired by his grandmother's recipes and the vibrant street food culture of India, set out to create a place where traditional flavors meet modern dining.

Today, FlavourHUB serves over 5,000 happy customers annually, offering 20+ dishes that celebrate the diversity and richness of Indian vegetarian cuisine.
